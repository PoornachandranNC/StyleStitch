How to use

  1. Point your camera at the plain area of the dress or fabric.
  2. Use one finger to drag the embroidery over the fabric.
  3. Use two fingers to pinch-zoom and rotate the design.
  4. Tap "Capture Screenshot" to save a preview photo.

Works on most phones:

  • Just allow camera access when the browser asks.
  • No ARCore / special hardware required.
  • Best experience on Chrome (Android) or Safari (iOS).

Note: On some devices you may need HTTPS for camera permission to work smoothly.

If the camera does not start, check browser permissions.
